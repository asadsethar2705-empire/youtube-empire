🤖 AI workflow — Instagram content in 1 hour/week
Monday morning batch (60 min total)
Step 1 (10 min): Ask Claude — "Write 7 Instagram captions for this week: 3 for Reels repurposed from YouTube Shorts, 2 for carousel posts about [AI tips / business tips], 2 motivational quotes. Include 20 hashtags for each. Niche: [yours]."
Step 2 (15 min): Create 2 carousel posts in Canva — 10 slides each — paste Claude's content
Step 3 (10 min): Create 7 Stories in Canva — use templates — add poll or question sticker
Step 4 (10 min): Download your 7 YouTube Shorts without watermark from CapCut — these become your Reels
Step 5 (15 min): Schedule everything in Buffer — set times for the whole week — done
Result: 7 Reels + 2 carousels + 35 Stories + 2 quote posts posted automatically all week. Zero daily work after Monday.
